# Packing Categories Reference

## Clothing

### Base Quantities (7-day trip, no laundry)
- Underwear: 7-8
- Socks: 7-8 pairs
- Tops: 5-6
- Bottoms: 3-4
- Sleepwear: 1-2

### Climate Adjustments

**Tropical/Hot (25°C+)**
- Lightweight, breathable fabrics (linen, cotton)
- Light colors to reflect heat
- Sun hat, sunglasses
- Swimwear
- Light rain jacket (tropical storms)

**Temperate (10-25°C)**
- Layering pieces
- Light jacket or cardigan
- Mix of short and long sleeves
- Comfortable walking shoes

**Cold (Below 10°C)**
- Thermal base layers
- Warm coat
- Scarf, gloves, hat
- Warm socks
- Waterproof boots

**Variable/Unpredictable**
- Versatile layers
- Packable rain jacket
- Items that mix and match

### Purpose-Specific

**Business**
- Formal attire (suits, dress shirts/blouses)
- Dress shoes
- Belt
- Tie/accessories

**Leisure**
- Casual comfortable clothing
- Athletic wear if active
- Swimwear for beach/pool destinations

**Adventure/Outdoor**
- Quick-dry fabrics
- Sturdy hiking boots
- Weather-appropriate layers
- Activity-specific gear

## Electronics

### Essential
- Phone & charger
- Power adapter (destination-specific)
- Portable battery pack

### Optional
- Laptop/tablet & chargers
- Camera & accessories
- E-reader
- Headphones
- Travel router

### Power Adapters by Region
- **North America:** Type A/B (120V)
- **Europe:** Type C/F (230V)
- **UK/Ireland:** Type G (230V)
- **Australia/NZ:** Type I (230V)
- **Japan:** Type A/B (100V)
- **China:** Type A/C/I (220V)

### Recommended

- Medical information card

## Health & Safety

### General
- Prescription medications (original packaging)
- Pain relievers
- Antihistamines
- Motion sickness medication
- Band-aids & basic first aid
- Hand sanitiser

### Destination-Specific Health Risks

**Tropical Regions (SE Asia, Africa, South America)**
- Mosquito repellent (DEET or Picaridin)
- Antimalarial medication
- Water purification tablets
- Oral rehydration salts
- Vaccinations: Hepatitis A/B, Typhoid, Yellow Fever (some regions)

**High Altitude (Andes, Himalayas, Tibet)**
- Altitude sickness medication (Acetazolamide)
- Extra hydration supplies
- Sunscreen (UV stronger at altitude)

**Remote Areas**
- Comprehensive first aid kit
- Emergency medication supplies
- Water purification system

**Common Vaccination Recommendations by Region**
- **Africa:** Yellow Fever, Typhoid, Hepatitis A/B, Meningitis
- **Southeast Asia:** Hepatitis A/B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis
- **South America:** Yellow Fever, Typhoid, Hepatitis A/B
- **South Asia:** Hepatitis A/B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis
- **Developed countries:** Routine vaccinations usually sufficient
